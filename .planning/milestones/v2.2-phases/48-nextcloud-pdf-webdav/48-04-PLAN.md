| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-48-11 | Elevation of Privilege | POST /pdf-export-config/trigger | high | mitigate | Handler ruft `permission_service.check_permission("admin", context)` VOR `tokio::spawn`; Test in Task 2 belegt Non-Admin=403. |
