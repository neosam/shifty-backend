## Release-Notes (Tag-Message v2.6.1, EN)

```
Bug Fixes:
- Voluntary hours target (Soll) now applies whole-week-out semantics on absence weeks:
  any ISO calendar week with at least one absence day of the salesperson (vacation,
  sick leave, unpaid leave — category-agnostic) contributes 0 to the Soll aggregate.
  This matches the semantics the weekly display has used since v2.6.0 and fixes the
  systematic overstatement of the Ist/Soll delta for volunteers with absence periods
  in the evaluated range.
- Contract-weeks denominator (ist_per_contract_week) excludes absence weeks:
  available voluntary weeks are counted correctly instead of being diluted by
  absence periods.
```
