## Sampling Strategy

**Continuous** (Nyquist Dimension 8b/8c): every task has an `<automated>`
verification step that runs in seconds — no watchAll, no 30s+ delays. The
plan-checker's Dimension-8 audit passes at all sub-criteria except 8e
(VALIDATION.md presence) — this file resolves that.

## Wave-0-Gaps (gaps that are intentionally not tested in 8.2)

- **Manual-Range vs. Heuristic provenance** in `absence_period_migration_source`:
  per **D-28**, both paths produce indistinguishable rows. No test verifies
  "which path produced this row" because no consumer cares.
- **Multi-row Manual-Range** (Bulk-Manual-Range): explicitly **out-of-scope**
  per CONTEXT.md. No bulk endpoint, no bulk modal, no test.
- **Live `category` mutation through the Modal**: per **D-31**, category is
  read-only. No mutation surface exists, no test.
- **Live `amount` editing through the Modal**: per **D-32**, amount is read-only
  (informational). The displayed value is the legacy `extra_hours.amount`; the
  Backend re-derives effective hours from `range + contract` server-side.
