<objective>
Implement VFA-01: in the live year-view (`booking_information.rs::get_weekly_summary`),
a volunteer with any absence overlapping a calendar week has their committed-voluntary
pledge (🎯) excluded from that week's `committed_voluntary_hours` (whole week to 0,
D-26-03), for all three absence categories (D-26-01). Wire `AbsenceService` into
`BookingInformationService` (no cycle). Holidays remain structurally untouched
(D-26-04). No snapshot bump (D-26-02).

Purpose: Volunteer vacation/absence no longer overstates planned availability in the
year-view — the committed-voluntary band reflects that an absent volunteer is not
available that week.
Output: Wired DI, the reduction logic + a testable pure overlap helper, and VFA-01
pure-helper unit tests.
</objective>
